Detach Xyz Material instead

Examples
Battlin' Boxer Lead Yoke

2 Level 4 "Battlin' Boxer" monsters
If a "Battlin' Boxer" monster(s) you control would be destroyed by battle or card effect, you can detach 1 material from this card instead of destroying 1 of those monsters. If a material(s) is detached from this card: This card gains 800 ATK.

Number 19: Freezadon

2 Level 5 monsters
Once per turn, if an Xyz Monster you control would activate an effect by detaching an Xyz Material(s), you can detach 1 Xyz Material from this card instead of 1 of those Xyz Materials.

Number 102: Star Seraph Sentry

3 Level 4 LIGHT monsters
Once per turn: You can detach 1 Xyz Material from this card, then target 1 face-up monster your opponent controls; halve its ATK, also, negate its effects. If this face-up card on the field would be destroyed, you can detach all of its Xyz Materials instead. If you do, any battle damage you take this turn is halved.

  • Effects of this type send the Xyz Materials to the graveyard by card effect[1].
    Example Question:
    A "Number 19: Freezadon" with "Shaddoll Beast" as an Xyz Material and a "Number 61: Volcasaurus" with no Xyz Material are both face-up in my Monster Zone. In this situation, I activate the effect of "Number 61: Volcasaurus", also applying the effect of "Number 19: Freezadon" to detach "Shaddoll Beast" in order to activate the effect. At this time, if "Shaddoll Beast" is sent to the Graveyard, can its effect that allows me to draw 1 card be activated?
    Answer:
    In this situation, the attached "Shaddoll Beast" is sent to the Graveyard by the effect of "Number 19: Freezadon" rather than being detached from "Number 61: Volcasaurus" as a cost to activate its effect. Since "Shaddoll Beast" was sent to the Graveyard by a card effect, you can activate its effect.
