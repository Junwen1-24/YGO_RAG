Cannot summon - Summon restrictions

Examples
Mozarta the Melodious Maestra

Once per turn: You can Special Summon 1 LIGHT Fairy-Type monster from your hand. You cannot Special Summon other monsters the turn you activate this effect, except for LIGHT monsters.
Power Filter

Neither player can Special Summon monsters with 1000 or less ATK.
Dimensional Barrier

Declare 1 monster card type (Ritual, Fusion, Synchro, Xyz, or Pendulum); for the rest of this turn, neither player can Special Summon monsters of the declared type, also negate the effects of all monsters of that type while they are on the field. You can only activate 1 "Dimensional Barrier" per turn.
  • The restriction of "you cannot Normal or Special Summon the turn you activate this card" only refers to monsters that were successfully Summoned (summon not negated). Same for "you can only Special Summon [X] once per turn" restrictions[1][2].
    Example Question:
    Ⓐ: If I successfully Special Summon "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon" using its listed procedure, can I activate "Rage with Eyes of Blue" later during the same turn?
    Ⓑ: If I have activated "Rage with Eyes of Blue" this turn, can I Special Summon "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon" from my hand?
    Ⓒ: If I attempt to Special Summon "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon" using its listed procedure, but that Special Summon is negated, can I activate "Rage with Eyes of Blue" later during the same turn?
    Ⓓ: If I activate "Rage with Eyes of Blue", but this activation, or its effect, is negated, can I still Special Summon "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon" from my hand this turn?
    Answer:
    Ⓐ: Yes, you can. Due to "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon"'s effect, its name becomes ""Blue-Eyes White Dragon"" while it is face-up in the Monster Zone, or in the Graveyard. Therefore, this is treated as you Special Summoning ""Blue-Eyes White Dragon"", and you can still activate "Rage with Eyes of Blue" this turn. (You can also activate the second effect of "Maiden of White", the second effect of "Legend of Justice Kaibaman", or the second effect of "The Ultimate Creature of Destruction" after Special Summoning "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon" this way.)
    Ⓑ: No, you cannot. Since the "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon" in your hand is not ""Blue-Eyes White Dragon"", you cannot Special Summon it after activating "Rage with Eyes of Blue".
    Ⓒ: Yes, you can. Since you did not successfully Special Summon "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon", you have not Special Summoned a monster except "Blue-Eyes White Dragon" this turn.
    Ⓓ①: If the activation of "Rage with Eyes of Blue" was negated, "you cannot Normal or Special Summon monsters the turn you activate this card, except ""Blue-Eyes White Dragon""" is not applicable. Therefore, you can still Special Summon "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon" from your hand.
    Ⓓ②: If the activation of "Rage with Eyes of Blue" is not negated, but only the activated effect is negated, or that effect is not applied for some reason, you will be unable to Special Summon "Blue-Eyes Alternative White Dragon" from your hand due to the "you cannot Normal or Special Summon monsters the turn you activate this card, except ""Blue-Eyes White Dragon""" text of "Rage with Eyes of Blue".
