Skip the next [X] Phase

Examples
Yata-Garasu

Cannot be Special Summoned. During the End Phase of the turn this card was Normal Summoned or flipped face-up: Return it to the hand. If this card inflicts battle damage to your opponent: Skip the Draw Phase of their next turn.
Runick Tip

Activate 1 of these effects, but skip your next Battle Phase after activation;
● Add 1 "Runick" card from your Deck to your hand, except "Runick Tip", then banish the top card of your opponent's Deck.
● Special Summon 1 "Runick" monster from your Extra Deck to the Extra Monster Zone.
You can only activate 1 "Runick Tip" per turn.

Reckless Greed

Draw 2 cards and skip your next 2 Draw Phases.
  • Multiple phase skipping effects of the same phase do not stack: if two or more effects are applying that say "skip the next [X] Phase" only one phase [X] will be skipped[1].
    Example Question:
    Multiple copies of "Grandsoil the Elemental Lord", "Moulinglacia the Elemental Lord", "Pyrorex the Elemental Lord", and/or "Windrose the Elemental Lord" are face-up in my Monster Zone. If "Dark Hole" is activated, so that these monsters are all destroyed simultaneously, how will their effects that skip the Battle Phase of my next turn be applied?
    Answer:
    In this situation, even if multiple copies of "Grandsoil the Elemental Lord", etc. are destroyed simultaneously, only the Battle Phase of your next turn will be skipped. (This is no different than having only a single copy leave the field.)
