Cannot attack unless do [A]

Examples
Mist Valley Falcon

Cannot declare an attack unless you return 1 card you control to the hand.
Panther Warrior

This card cannot declare an attack unless you Tribute 1 monster.
Gravekeeper's Servant

Unless your opponent sends 1 card from the top of his/her Deck to the Graveyard, he/she cannot declare an attack.
  • These are continuous effects, but the action performed [A] is a cost and it is treated as so[1].
    Example Question:
    If "Peten the Dark Clown" is Tributed to declare an attack with "Panther Warrior", can "Peten the Dark Clown"'s effect be activated?
    Answer:
    Yes, it can. (In this case, "Peten the Dark Clown"'s effect will activate in response to "Panther Warrior"'s attack declaration.)
